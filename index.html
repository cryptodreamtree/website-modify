Definition of Lunamaximalist

Before explaining the Luna Maximalist, I will establish the concept for those who do not know about Luna. Luna is a coin to help peg the UST. UST is an algorithmic stablecoin. For example, it is the principle of making the price of one dollar set by the arbitrage trader. In one of the situations, if UST is $1.05, swap UST for Luna and make a profit of $0.05 to match $1. The opposite is also true. Those who believe in this mechanism and the increase in LUNA price through the incineration of LUNA through the expansion of the base of UST are called Luna maximalists.
